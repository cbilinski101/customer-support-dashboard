# Customer Support Insights

## Overview
This analysis explores customer support ticket data to identify trends in workload, performance, and customer satisfaction.

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Ticket Volume and Status
- Most tickets are successfully resolved.
- A smaller portion remains open, indicating ongoing workload.

**Insight:**
The team is handling volume well, but monitoring open tickets is important to prevent backlog growth.

---

### 2. High-Priority Tickets
- High-priority tickets appear frequently.
- These tickets are generally resolved faster than others.

**Insight:**
The team prioritizes urgent issues effectively, which helps maintain customer trust.

---

### 3. Ticket Categories
- Technical and Billing issues are the most common.
- General inquiries occur less frequently.

**Insight:**
Training and resources should focus on Technical and Billing categories to improve efficiency.

---

### 4. Agent Performance
- Some agents respond faster than others.
- Faster response times are often linked to higher customer satisfaction.

**Insight:**
Sharing best practices from top-performing agents could improve overall team performance.

---

### 5. Resolution Time
- Resolution times vary depending on ticket complexity and category.
- Some categories take longer to resolve than others.

**Insight:**
Identifying slow categories can help optimize workflows or introduce better tools.

---

### 6. Customer Satisfaction
- Higher satisfaction scores are associated with faster response and resolution times.
- Lower scores may indicate delays or complex issues.

**Insight:**
Improving response time is likely to increase overall customer satisfaction.

---

### 7. Daily Ticket Trends
- Ticket volume fluctuates by day.
- Some days experience higher support demand.

**Insight:**
Staffing levels could be adjusted based on peak days to reduce wait times.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve response time consistency across agents
- Focus training on high-volume categories (Technical, Billing)
- Monitor open tickets to prevent backlog
- Use data to align staffing with peak demand
- Share strategies from high-performing agents

---

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how customer support data can be used to generate actionable insights that improve efficiency, performance, and customer experience.
